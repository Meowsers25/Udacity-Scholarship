Snowy Owl

The snowy owl is a ground nester.

  • Scientific Name: Bubo scandiacus
  • Average Length: 52 - 71 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 10 years
  • Habitat: The Arctic tundra of Alaska, Northern Canada, and Eurasia

The snowy owl is a large, white owl of the typical owl family. Males are almost all white, while females have more flecks of black plumage. Juvenile snowy owls have black feathers until they turn white.
